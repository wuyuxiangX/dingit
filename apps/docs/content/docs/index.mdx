**Dingit** is a self-hosted notification system with three first-class clients
(Server, Flutter App, Go CLI). Unlike a plain push service, every Dingit
notification carries **Actions** and an optional **callback hook** — one tap on
the App bell, and your script hears back.

## Why Dingit?

| You have | Dingit gives you |
|----------|------------------|
| A long-running script (training, backup, crawl) | A bell that actually tells you when it's done |
| A CI pipeline that sometimes fails silently | A push with a "Rerun" action that calls back into CI |
| A home-lab with 10 places that might need attention | One card stack, three devices, synced state |
| Strong feelings about data ownership | Your PostgreSQL, your server, zero third-party lock-in |

## The three clients

- **Server** (Go) — REST + WebSocket, PostgreSQL backed, Prometheus metrics, version-migrated schema.
- **App** (Flutter) — iOS, Android, Web, macOS. Card-stack UI, editorial type, warm-paper aesthetic.
- **CLI** (Go) — single binary, `curl | sh` installable, script-friendly subcommands.

One notification, three surfaces, one coherent model. That's the whole
product.
